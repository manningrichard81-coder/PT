The Program

7-Week Iron Protocol

Seven structured phases designed to rewire your habits and rebuild your body. Everything you need — nothing you don't.

$700 Complete 7-week package
Week 01

Assessment & Foundation

Full body composition analysis, movement screening, goal setting, and baseline strength testing. We find where you are and map where you're going.

Week 02

Movement Mechanics

Drill the fundamentals — deadlift, squat, press, row. Perfect form before heavy load. Build the neural pathways that prevent injury and maximise output.

Week 03

Nutrition Reset

Strip back the processed rubbish. Build a structured eating framework — macro targets, meal timing, whole-food foundations. Your gut and your energy will thank you.

Week 04

Progressive Loading

Now we build. Structured progressive overload across compound movements. Volume, intensity, and recovery managed week by week. You'll feel the difference.

Week 05

Metabolic Conditioning

Beach runs, battle ropes, rowing intervals, ocean swims. We layer conditioning onto your strength base — building the engine, not just the chassis.

Week 06

Recovery & Resilience

Cold exposure, sauna protocols, sleep optimisation, and stress management. The body rebuilds during rest — we make sure it does it right.
